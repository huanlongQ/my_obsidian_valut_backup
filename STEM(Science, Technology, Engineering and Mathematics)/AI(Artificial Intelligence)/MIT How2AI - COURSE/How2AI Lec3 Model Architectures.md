## data invariance and data equivariance 

1)  data invariance 

$$
\begin{align}
f(g \circ x)=f(x)
\end{align}
$$
2) data equivariance 
$$
\begin{align}
f(g\circ x) = g\circ f(x)
\end{align}
$$
